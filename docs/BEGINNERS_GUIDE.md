## üéØ Getting Started

### What is ELIDA?
ELIDA (Enhanced Learning Investment Decision Advisor) is an AI-powered tool that helps you analyze stocks based on:
- **Fundamentals** (P/E ratio, revenue, profits)
- **Market Trends** (price movements, volume)
- **Risk Assessment** (potential downsides)
- **Your Personal Profile** (risk tolerance, investment goals)

## üìñ Essential Concepts

### Key Terms You'll See in ELIDA

| Term | What It Means |
|------|---------------|
| **P/E Ratio** | Price-to-Earnings ratio. Lower = cheaper, but context matters |
| **Market Cap** | Total company value (Share Price √ó Total Shares) |
| **52-Week High/Low** | Highest and lowest prices in the past year |
| **Volume** | Number of shares traded - higher means more active |
| **Beta** | Volatility measure. Beta > 1 = more volatile than market |
| **Match Score** | ELIDA's personalized rating (0-100%) based on your profile |

## üöÄ Using ELIDA Effectively

### Step 1: Set Up Your Profile
- Go to **Profile** page
- Define your risk tolerance (Conservative/Moderate/Aggressive)
- Set your investment horizon (Short/Medium/Long term)
- Add sectors to avoid based on personal preferences

### Step 2: Analyze Stocks
- Enter any stock ticker (e.g., `RELIANCE.NS`, `AAPL`)
- Review the Match Score and AI analysis
- Read each agent's perspective (Quant, Macro, Philosopher, Regret)

### Step 3: Make Informed Decisions
- Use ELIDA's insights alongside your own research
- Consider the risk warnings seriously
- Track your analyses in the History section

## ‚ùì Common Questions

**Q: What does a Match Score of 75% mean?**
A: It means the stock aligns 75% with your investor profile. Higher is better for YOUR situation.

**Q: Why did the Philosopher agent give a low score?**
A: The Philosopher evaluates ethical alignment and long-term business quality. A low score may indicate ESG concerns.

**Q: Can I trust the AI recommendations?**
A: Use them as one input among many. AI can analyze data quickly but can't predict the future.
